# Relevant Data

Data goes in here!

## General Transit Feed Specification (GTFS)

GTFS data from [transit.land](https://transit.land). Script saves latest and oldest available (if less than 12 archives) or the 12th latest.

## LEHD Origin-Destination Employment Statistics (LODES)

LODES data from entire state, latest available is 2022.

## American Community Survey (ACS)

ACS 2023 (5-Year Estimates) at census tract level and for overall city, from Social Explorer. Metadata available in [socialexplorer.md](./socialexplorer.md)

The report is also available [on Social Explorer](https://www.socialexplorer.com/reports/socialexplorer/en/report/ea1399b4-aecc-11f0-81d8-a3d2f315e9eb).
